Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process withdrawals?

Withdrawal requests are processed manually by our team. Typically, requests are processed within 1-3 business days. During high volume periods, processing may take longer.

What is the minimum withdrawal amount?

The standard minimum withdrawal amount is $0.90 USD. However, if you've used a referral code or have referred friends, your minimum withdrawal amount is reduced to $0.10 USD.

Why isn't my task showing as completed?

Tasks may take a few minutes to verify. If a task isn't showing as completed after 5 minutes, please try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, contact support with the task ID.

How do I get the 1.10x bonus?

New users automatically receive a 1.10x earnings bonus for 24 hours after account creation. This bonus applies to all tasks completed during this period.
