Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 15, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to FaucetClicks ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website at faucetclicks.infinityfree.me.

Important Note for Minors: If you are under 18 years old, please get permission from your parent or guardian before sharing any personal information with us.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a username.
  • Wallet Information: You may provide a cryptocurrency wallet address for withdrawals.
  • Referral Information: If you use a referral code or share your own, we collect that information.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our service, including tasks completed and points earned.
  • Device Information: We collect your device type, browser, and IP address for security purposes.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To process your withdrawal requests
  • To verify task completion for earning points
  • To communicate with you about your account
  • To improve our service and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

Security Note: We never share your wallet address with third parties. Your wallet address is only used to process withdrawals.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Practices

  • All sensitive data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • Authentication credentials are stored using industry-standard hashing
  • Regular security audits are performed on our systems
  • Access to user data is limited to authorized personnel only

However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. We will take steps to delete such information.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
